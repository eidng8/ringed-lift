# Conclusion (Tentative)

The above provides a conceptual overview of the Space Ringed-Elevation project, aiming to outline the overall picture and key technological contours. Detailed technical solutions, engineering plans, and financial models will be gradually elaborated in subsequent specialized volumes.

At this stage, this overview focuses solely on engineering and economic aspects, without addressing international governance structures. Once the project enters the substantive promotion phase, a multilateral cooperation mechanism and legal framework must be separately established.

As a long-term vision, this document proposes a preliminary principle for operation and returns: after the Space Ringed-Elevation is completed, it will be operated uniformly by the United Nations, and all proceeds will belong to the United Nations. Before the construction costs are fully recovered, the United Nations will settle principal and interest in installments to all investing countries according to their actual investment proportions. The specific investment ratios, interest rate benchmarks, settlement cycles, and supervision mechanisms are to be determined through subsequent dedicated consultations.
