### Positioning of the Volumes

> This appendix focuses on **extreme operating condition analysis and forward-looking technical analysis** that fall outside the scope of routine design in the Space Ring Elevator Project and the Lunar Industrial System. It does not establish engineering baselines; instead, it records the technical difficulties, bottlenecks, and potential engineering solutions identified in scenarios such as extreme failures, multi-physics coupling, ultra-long-term operation, and extraterrestrial manufacturing. The purpose of this appendix is to provide directional reference for future design iterations, technology breakthroughs, and forward-looking research.
>
> The core distinction between this appendix and the Main Volume and Appendixes I and II is: the Main Volume and Appendixes I and II record the "baseline design of the current scheme," whereas this appendix records "problems beyond the baseline that have been set aside or deferred for future resolution." It does not cover schemes that have already been validated within the baseline design.
